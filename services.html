IN-HOUSE SERVICES

Time management is paramount in a busy office environment. We are experts at setting up the right brewing systems for all types of busy office environments. Such technology, be it via bean-to-cup or traditional espresso machines, will deliver favourites such as Espresso, Latte, Cappuccinos, Mocha or your Prime Velvet (flat white) without leaving your premises, thereby helping your organisation boost its productivity. We are available to come and discuss your particular coffee needs at any time. For larger organisations, having their own in-house coffee bar is the most effective solution. This is akin to having your favourite coffee beverages right within your office environment. Over the years, we have helped many organisations develop, run and operate their in-house coffee services, both in the City of London and Canary Wharf. ​
